About Dauin

Dauin is a municipality on the southeastern coast of Negros Oriental known for world-class snorkeling and dive sites, warm community hospitality, and agricultural heritage. Local conservation groups work with communities to protect coral reefs and marine biodiversity.

  • Rich marine biodiversity — home to sea turtles and colorful reefs
  • Family-friendly beaches and eco-tours
  • Festivals, local cuisine and home stays
